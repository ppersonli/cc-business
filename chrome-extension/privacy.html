Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data Collection

ScreenMind does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not operate servers that receive your information.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.sync. They are never sent to our servers or any third party. Keys are only transmitted directly from your browser to the AI provider you configure (OpenAI or Anthropic).

Page Content

Screenshot data you choose to analyze is sent exclusively to your selected AI provider (OpenAI or Anthropic) via their official APIs. No content passes through intermediate servers.

Analytics & Tracking

  • No analytics
  • No tracking pixels
  • No third-party data sharing
  • No cookies

Subscription

If you subscribe to a premium plan, subscription status is verified using an anonymous user ID generated locally. No personally identifiable information is associated with this ID.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published on this page with a new revision date.
